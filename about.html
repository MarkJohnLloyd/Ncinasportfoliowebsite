Project Showcase:

Our project is a prototype water filtration system using sensors and an Arduino Uno to purify tap water. This ensures students have access to clean, safe drinking water, aligning with the Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) of Clean Water and Sanitation. By leveraging modern technology, we address the immediate need for safe water and contribute to long-term health benefits.

Significance of Clean Water and Sanitation

Clean water and sanitation are essential for health and well-being. SDG 6 aims to ensure everyone has access to safe water and sanitation, which is crucial for preventing diseases and promoting sustainable development. Our prototype water filtration system purifies tap water, providing students with clean and safe drinking water. This project aligns with SDG 6 by addressing the need for accessible clean water. Our project, a prototype water filtration system, directly aligns with SDG 6. This system is designed to purify water from tap sources, ensuring that students have access to clean and safe water. This initiative not only addresses the immediate need for safe drinking water but also contributes to long-term health and educational benefits.
